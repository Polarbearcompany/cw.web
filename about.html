Video games have made a big contribution to the entertainment industry and so far the gaming industry shows no signs of slowing down. The reason probably lies in the fact that games are played by everyone, regardless of gender and age. So today, video game companies have become influential players in the technology sector.

Square Bear Holdings is a Uzbek video game holding company, best known for its game constructor, Ruun, and IseeU role-playing video game franchises, among numerous others. Several of them have sold over 10 million copies worldwide, with the game constructor franchise alone selling 144 million, the Ruun franchise selling 78 million and the IseeU franchise selling 30 million. The Square Bear headquarters are in the Alisher Navoi Square Building in NBU, Tashkent. Outside of video game publishing and development which the group is more known, Square Bear also is in the business of merchandise, amusement and publication of manga. The company employs some 4600 workers worldwide through its base operations and subsidiaries.
